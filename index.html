World happiness index and what affects it

The data is based on 157 countries out of 195 countries in the world.

This might be caused by the non existing data of smaller countries such as Vatican City and Holy Sea and other countries such as North Korea and Cuba.

The map above is a world map that shows how happy the country is depending on the colour. The brighter the colour, the happier the country is.
People might have the false understanding that USA which ranks 13th in terms of happiness is the happiest country in the world as all of the movies are usually filmed in USA which shows their wealth in
cities such as New York, Los Angeles and San Fransisco. However, they do not show the other side of the community such as the homeless and the people who are struggling to even pay their rent.
The happiest country in the world is Denmark. The reasons why the Danes is so happy is because of their social equality and their community spirit. They also have tuition-free education
which might contribute to their happiness. One survey says that they are more than happy to pay taxes as they have a lot of trust in their government and they are happy with how the
